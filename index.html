Our Story

A Studio Built on Tiny Wonder.

Sulutopi began at a kitchen table, when our founder watched her daughter light up the moment a brush touched water. The art sets on the shelf were chalky, dull, and forgettable. So she made her own.

Today, every Sulutopi set is still poured by hand in small batches — using artisan pigments, premium paper, and the kind of care that turns a rainy afternoon into a memory.

Curious Minds

Questions, Answered.

What ages are Sulutopi sets for?

Our sets are designed for ages 4 and up, with three tiers — Little Explorer (4+), Dreamer Deluxe (6+), and Young Studio (8+) — each scaled to suit growing skills.

Are the paints really safe?

Yes. Every pigment is non-toxic, washable, and independently lab-tested to meet ASTM D-4236 and EN 71-3 safety standards. Safe for sensitive skin and gentle on clothes.

How long do the watercolor pans last?

With regular use, expect 6–12 months of joyful painting per set. Pans rehydrate beautifully — even after months in a drawer, they wake up vibrant.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to over 40 countries. Free shipping on orders over $40 within the US, and tracked delivery on every international order.

What's your return policy?

If your set doesn't spark joy within 30 days, return it for a full refund. No questions, no fuss. We stand behind every box.
