Curious Minds

Questions, Answered.

Are your cards made with AI?

Absolutely not. Every single Sulutopi card is illustrated by professional human graphic designers. We believe children deserve real art, made by real artists.

Are the watercolor paints safe for kids?

Yes. Our paints are CE-certified to European safety standards — non-toxic, washable, and gentle on sensitive skin and clothes.

Can I buy cards, paints, or brush pens separately?

Yes — every component of our sets is also available individually. Restock just the cards, grab an extra refillable brush pen, or top up your paint pans whenever you need.

Can you create custom card themes for us?

We love custom commissions. Reach out at info@sulutopi.com with your idea — birthdays, schools, brand collaborations — and our designers will craft a one-of-a-kind set just for you.

What ages is Sulutopi for?

Our sets are designed for ages 4 and up, with three tiers — Little Explorer (4+), Dreamer Deluxe (6+), and Young Studio (8+) — each scaled to suit growing skills.

What's your return policy?

If your set doesn't spark joy within 30 days, return it for a full refund. No questions, no fuss. We stand behind every box.
